How it works

1) Connect your accounts

Link your existing broker so you can see your real holdings and performance in one clean view. No new trading account required.

2) Build or join a crew

Create private groups with mates or join communities around themes you care about – gold, small caps, ETFs, whatever fits your style.

3) Follow the feed

Our MatesFeed surface market headlines, ASX announcements and discussion, with plain-English AI summaries and context for your watchlist.

4) Decide in one place

Share ideas, react to news, compare portfolios and track how everyone is going – then place trades back with your broker when you’re ready.

Safety & data, front and centre

  • Read-only connections to your broker for the MVP — we don’t move money or execute trades.
  • No financial product advice. We surface information and tools; you make the decisions.
  • Data encryption in transit & at rest, and strict least-privilege access internally.
  • Clear audit trails for group activity, posts and shared ideas.

How MatesInvest fits

  1. MatesInvest app (social, summaries, groups)
  2. Broker & data providers (holdings, pricing, news)
  3. You execute trades with your broker as normal

FAQ

Is MatesInvest a broker?

No. MatesInvest is a social and research companion. You connect your existing broker, and you continue to execute trades with them.

Do you give financial advice?

No. We don’t provide financial product advice or discretionary management. We help you see information, summaries and what your mates are doing in one place.

Will you ever touch client funds or custody?

The current vision focuses on read-only connections and social features. Any future move into execution or custody would only happen with the right licences and partners.

What makes MatesInvest different?

Most apps are either pure brokers or pure “finfluencer” content. MatesInvest sits in the middle – real holdings, real mates, and AI-powered summaries that cut through the noise.
