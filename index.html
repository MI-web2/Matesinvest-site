How it works

1) Connect your accounts

Securely link your existing broker so MatesInvest can read your holdings and performance in one clean view. Read-only for the MVP – no new trading account required.

2) Build or join a crew

Create private groups with mates or join communities around themes you care about – gold, small caps, ETFs, whatever fits your style.

3) Follow the feed

Our MatesFeed surfaces market headlines, ASX announcements and discussion, with plain-English AI summaries and context for your watchlist and holdings.

4) Decide in one place

Share ideas, react to news, compare portfolios and track how everyone is going – then place trades back with your broker when you’re ready.

Safety & data, front and centre

  • Read-only connections to your broker for the MVP — we don’t move money or execute trades.
  • No financial product advice — we surface information and tools; you make the decisions.
  • Data encryption in transit & at rest, and strict least-privilege access internally.
  • Clear audit trails for group activity, posts and shared ideas.

How MatesInvest fits

  1. MatesInvest app (social, summaries, groups)

FAQ

Is MatesInvest giving financial advice?

No. MatesInvest is a technology and information platform. We don’t provide financial product advice, recommendations or discretionary management. We help you see information and activity more clearly so you can make your own decisions.

Do you execute trades or hold my assets?

No. For the MVP, MatesInvest connects to your existing broker in a read-only way. You still place trades and hold assets with your broker as normal.

Can I join multiple groups?

Yes. Your dashboard will show each group’s discussions, watchlists and shared ideas, so you can keep different themes or circles separate.

Who is MatesInvest designed for?

Australian retail investors who already use a broker and want a smarter, more social way to track ideas, news and performance with their mates — without handing over control of their account.
