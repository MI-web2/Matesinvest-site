How it works

1) Create a private pool

Start a pool, set simple rules, invite your mates via link.

2) Fund & track ownership

Deposit funds; we track each member’s beneficial share with a clean ledger.

3) Propose & vote

Any member can propose a trade. The group votes; majority wins.

4) Broker executes

Trades executed under a broker sub‑account; holdings update automatically.

FAQ

Is MatesInvest giving financial advice?

No. We’re a technology platform. We don’t provide financial product advice or discretionary management.

How do withdrawals work?

Sell down your share of the pool per group rules. Settlement is handled through the broker.

Can I join multiple groups?

Yes. Your dashboard shows each pool’s holdings, votes, and cash independently.

Is this a managed investment scheme?

The MVP is designed around sub‑accounts + beneficial ownership to avoid MIS classification; at scale we may migrate to an MIS.
